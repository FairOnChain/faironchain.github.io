## Blockchain Datasets

FairOnChain provides **open access to comprehensive blockchain datasets** following the [**FAIR principles**](https://www.go-fair.org/fair-principles/){:target="_blank"}. All data is collected from our own nodes and **updated daily**, ensuring reliable and up-to-date information for researchers, developers, and the community.

Our datasets cover multiple blockchain networks, providing complete on-chain data including blocks, transactions, smart contracts, and historical information.

## FAIR Principles Implementation

Our datasets are designed following the FAIR principles to ensure maximum usability and reproducibility:

</div>

<div class="wrapper" markdown="1">
<div class="col-1-of-2 text-center" markdown="1">
<span class="la la-search"></span>
### Findable

All datasets are cataloged with rich metadata and accessible through our public file browser. Each blockchain has its dedicated directory with clear structure.
</div>

<div class="col-1-of-2 text-center" markdown="1">
<span class="la la-unlock"></span>
### Accessible

Datasets are freely accessible without authentication requirements. Direct download links are provided for all blockchain data.
</div>

<div class="col-1-of-2 text-center" markdown="1">
<span class="la la-exchange-alt"></span>
### Interoperable

Data is provided in standard formats compatible with major blockchain analysis tools and frameworks. Schema.org metadata ensures machine readability.
</div>

<div class="col-1-of-2 text-center" markdown="1">
<span class="la la-recycle"></span>
### Reusable

Clear licensing and comprehensive documentation enable researchers and developers to reuse our datasets for various applications and analyses.
</div>

## Data Collection & Updates

All blockchain data is collected from **our own dedicated nodes** that we host and maintain. This ensures:

<ul style="list-style-position: inside; padding-left: 0;">
<li><strong>Data Integrity</strong>: Direct access to blockchain networks without third-party intermediaries</li>
<li><strong>Reliability</strong>: Continuous monitoring and maintenance of our infrastructure</li>
<li><strong>Freshness</strong>: Daily updates to keep datasets current with the latest blockchain state</li>
<li><strong>Completeness</strong>: Full historical data from genesis blocks to present day</li>
</ul>
